Hackathon Management Ranks

Admiral

The highest rank in hackathon management, providing overall leadership.

This is a non-promotable, leadership-appointed role.

Vice Admiral

The second-highest rank, acting as deputy to the Admiral.

This is a non-promotable, leadership-appointed role.

Chief Engineer

Oversees the engineering team and technical implementation.

This is a non-promotable, leadership-appointed role.

Participant Ranks

Cadet

The entry-level rank for all new hackathon participants.

Awarded upon successful registration.

Ensign

Awarded for demonstrating exceptional skill and commitment.

20 available positions.

Lieutenant

Requires prerequisite knowledge and promotion from Ensign.

10 available positions.

Lieutenant Commander

Requires prerequisite knowledge and promotion from Lieutenant.

5 available positions.

Commander

The highest participant rank, requiring promotion from Lt. Commander.

1 available position.

Mentors and Sponsors

Judge

Evaluates projects and selects winners.

This is an invitation-only role.

Subject Matter Expert

Provides expert knowledge in specific subject areas.

This is an invitation-only role.

Generic Mentor

Guides and supports participants throughout the hackathon.

This is an invitation-only role.
